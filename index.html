Our Story

Built in a Kitchen.
Fuelled by Purpose.

Y Fit Kitchen was founded by a fitness enthusiast who got tired of choosing between eating well and eating conveniently. We built a kitchen around one belief: healthy food should be effortless, delicious, and accessible to every athlete.

Today we serve hundreds of athletes across the region with chef-crafted meals that are macro-tracked, food-safe certified, and packed in eco-friendly materials.
